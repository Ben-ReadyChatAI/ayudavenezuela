# Ayuda Venezuela — Research & Sources

**Purpose:** citations behind every fact and link shown on **ayudavenezuela.lat**, for review by the project administrator before wider distribution.

**Compiled:** 2026-06-27 (~21:30 UTC), by automated multi-source research + independent verification.
**Primary anchor:** USGS earthquake API (pulled directly). Casualty/damage figures cross-checked across ≥2 sources each.

> ⚠️ **All casualty and damage figures are preliminary and rising. Re-verify before each publish / before printing emergency phone numbers.** Confidence is tagged HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW.

## 1. The event — what happened

A **doublet**: two large quakes **39 seconds apart** on **Wednesday 24 June 2026**.

| Fact | Value | Source | Confidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foreshock | **M7.2**, 22:04:33 UTC (18:04 VET), depth 20.3 km, 10.436°N 68.528°W | [USGS us6000t7zc](https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/us6000t7zc) | HIGH |
| Mainshock | **M7.5**, 22:05:11 UTC (18:05 VET), depth 10 km, 10.435°N 68.472°W | [USGS us6000t7zp](https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/us6000t7zp) | HIGH |

| Most-affected | **La Guaira (Vargas)** & **Caracas**, plus Yaracuy, Carabobo, Falcón, Aragua, Miranda | [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/6/26/venezuela-earthquakes-why-is-caracas-so-vulnerable), Wikipedia | HIGH |
